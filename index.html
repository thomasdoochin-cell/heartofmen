Hello There, I'm Thomas

In my early 20s, I founded and ran a successful startup. I ended up selling the company after 8 years because I felt I was ultimately abandoning something precious in who I really was in my leadership.

I fled to the other end of the spectrum: 22 acres with a solar-powered, spring-fed abode in the Appalachian Mountains. I wrote the best poetry in my life in these years, but I ultimately felt stagnant, fragmented, and self-imploding as I had abandoned my ambition.

My life the past several years has been an experiment to take courageous, inspired action on behalf of what's actually most important. It has created the life I most deeply long for.

It's through this lens that I look out on the world and see:

1) Powerful men with impressive skillsets and initiatives who are not living what's actually most important to them OR

2) Brothers awake to the deeper currents of Life unable to take bold, consistent action in service.

Living Leadership is the bridge of these two worlds.

It's how I created a life where I live in the forest, raise our own meat, and spend abundant time with my wife, son, and parents while running a growing business from my dharma that I believe contributes to the renaissance of our culture.

Over the next 8 months, you are going to get really good at what matters most to you. In doing so, you're going to create the future you've been waiting for.

Our Rhythm

You are going to learn how to masterfully lead yourself on behalf of what's most important to create the world you most deeply desire.

Our journey revolves around three components: capacity, initiation, and collaboration. Leadership capacities are the skills, practices, attunement, and perspectives you will cultivate to lead on behalf of what's most important. Initiations are the passages the soul calls forth from the personality to become the man trustworthy of the vision at hand. And collaboration within the brotherhood is what makes the previously uncharted path now inevitable.

We will move through four thresholds together over these eight months: darkness, desire, vision, and service. During each of these thresholds, our journey will be structured about masterfully-crafted capacities, initiations, and collaborations.

By the end of these 8 months, "maybe, one day I'll finally…" is going to become "Holy shit, no training wheels, LOOK AT ME GO"
