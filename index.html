Hello There, I'm Thomas

In my early 20s, I founded and ran a successful startup. I ended up selling the company after 8 years because I felt I was ultimately abandoning something precious in who I really was in my leadership.

I fled to the other end of the spectrum: 22 acres with a solar-powered, spring-fed abode in the Appalachian Mountains. I wrote the best poetry in my life in these years, but I ultimately felt stagnant, fragmented, and self-imploding as I had abandoned my ambition.

My life the past several years has been an experiment to take courageous, inspired action, on behalf of what's actually most important.

It's through this lens that I look out on the world and see:

1) Powerful men with impressive skillsets and initiatives who are not living what's actually most important to them OR

2) Brothers awake to the deeper currents of Life unable to take bold, consistent action in service.

Living Leadership is the bridge of these two worlds.

Over the next 8 months, you are going to lead yourself into a life built around what's actually most important to you. In doing so, you're going to create the future worthy of our sons and daughters.

Before we go further, I need you to know:

This brotherhood will not integrate all of your shadows. Nor will you leave these 8 months an enlightened man indefinitely beyond collapse, numbness, or distraction. You will still have bad days and hard moments.

But what I can promise: you will know what's most important and have the clarity, capacity, and courage to take consistent action on its behalf.

This is not the "men's work" you'll find in other places (I don't even call Heart of Men "men's work.") This is not "paint your face and get primal." Nor is it Robert Bly's mythopoetics. We don't sit in a fluorescent lit room in folding chairs and talk about our feelings. And it's not a space where men get together to be spiritually serious.

This journey feels much more like that hike you've always wanted to go in with your best dude friends to that mountaintop meadow. Where at the top you're feasted with honey wine and fire-braised Elk that you know you've earned.

Our Rhythm

You are going to consistently move towards what's most important. You're going to learn to meet—and then ride—the dragons that present themselves along the way. You're going to fall down—more times than you think you want to—and learn to pick yourself and one another back up.

You are going to do scary things you've been waiting a decade to do and find that not only don't they kill you, they bring you enormous momentum. You're going to build a life where your time, money, and attention flows to what's actually most important. And in all this, together, we're going to create a new future.

By the end of these 8 months, "maybe, one day I'll finally…" is going to become "Holy shit, no training wheels, LOOK AT ME GO"
